What are stocks?

Stocks are shares in the ownership of a company, held by investors who, in turn, gain rights to dividends and voting privileges in the company.

Why invest?

Investing can build wealth over time through returns, enable financial independence, and offer potential tax advantages, supporting long-term financial goals.

03

Why budget?

Budgeting helps manage finances effectively, ensuring bills are paid, savings grow, and unnecessary debt is avoided, aiding in financial stability.
