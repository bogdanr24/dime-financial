Frequently Asked Questions

Dime is a financial advisor with many features available for you to use. It can give suggestions on investing, budgeting, and other financial situations.

You can use Dime's budgeting tab for this need. All it will take is for you to fill out a small survey. As a result, you will receive some insight into your finances and a visualized chart with how you are spending your money.

Since Dime does not need any confidential information, you can be sure that you will be safe. Dime also does not save information so you can be sure that no one else has access to your personal financial information.

You can start a conversation through our Chatbot tab. Simply enter your question in the input box and you will receive a response within seconds. You can also try one of our fill questions on the left side if you are unsure where to start.

It is up to your discretion how much data you want to provide to Dime. Our analysis recommend basic background information such as place of living, gross income and age/occupation as reliable starter points. The more information is given, the more accurate the response can be. Be sure not to share information that can lead to danger of your identity.

We recommend refreshing the page whenever you encounter a problem with Dime. Aside from that, you can also send in a bug report so that we may further optimize Dime accordingly to your needs.

The suggestions that are given by Dime are not a financial advice. We are not responsible for any damages that might occur.
